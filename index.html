DOLIA
PETS AR

A revolutionary mobile game powered by Internet Computer Protocol (ICP) blockchain technology on the backend, allowing you to take care of your very own virtual Dolia Pet in a whole new way. With the security and decentralized nature of the ICP blockchain, you can trust that your virtual pet is being taken care of in the most secure way possible.

As a dedicated pet owner in Dolia Pets AR, you'll be responsible for feeding, playing, and keeping your Dolia Pet happy and healthy.

With AR technology, you'll be able to see your Dolia Pet come to life in your own environment, making the experience feel even more real.

ACTIVITIES

In the game, you'll have access to a wide range of activities and items to keep your Dolia Pet entertained. You can play with toys, groom your cat, and even take them on virtual adventures. As you progress through the game, you'll be able to unlock new items and customizations to keep things interesting.

In the Dolia Pet AR game, a play-to-earn mode might involve players completing tasks and activities to earn virtual currency or items that can be used to care for their virtual Dolia Pet.

For example, players might be able to earn rewards for playing with their cat, feeding them, or completing mini-games. These rewards could then be used to purchase items or customizations for the player's virtual pet.
